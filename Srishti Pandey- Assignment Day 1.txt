1.) Find the time complexity for the following scenarios
a.) 
  for(i=1;i<=n;i++)
    for(j=i;i<=n;j++)
      printf("Hi");

Answer:
      Time complexity= O(n^2)

b.) 
  for(i=1;i<=n;i*=3)
    for(j=1;i<=n;j++)
      printf("Hello");

Answer:
      Time complexity= O(nlog3(n))  (log of base 3)
